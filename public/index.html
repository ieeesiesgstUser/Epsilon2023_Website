Epsilon is three-day Academic Convention scheduled from the 30th September to 2nd of October where international speakers and industry expert from our established theme of 'The Galaxy of Intelligence' will join in. They will share their knowledge and expertise through a series of interactive webinars and hands-on workshop as well as panel discussion. The theme revolves around the large domain of Artificial Intelligence, with a primary focus on being well-versed in AI. It refers to the tactics, tools, and individuals employed to protect a company's dynamic. The theme "The Galaxy of Intelligence" encompasses AI concepts in multiple domains such as Nero AI, Computer Vision and Deep Space Communication. Our goal is to traverse this large domain, beginning at the beginning and working our way up to the conceptual level.
